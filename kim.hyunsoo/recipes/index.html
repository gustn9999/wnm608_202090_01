Avocado Salad

Materials

  • Spring Mix
  • Pepper
  • Avocado
  • Grape Tomato

Process

  • Place spring mix in the bowl.
  • Put some washed grape tomatoes on the top.
  • Slice avocado evenly and put it on the top.
  • Sprinkle peppers and done!
